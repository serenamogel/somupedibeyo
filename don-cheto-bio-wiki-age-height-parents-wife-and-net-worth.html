Don Cheto Biography

Don Cheto born Juan Carlos Razo is a fictional Mexican American radio and television personality widely known in Mexican American pop culture. Contrary to popular belief Don Cheto is actually portrayed by Juan Razo who is very young compared to the 65-year-old character of Don Cheto.

He is the host of El Show de Don Cheto an hour-long Spanish language variety show on Channel 62 in Los Angeles and shown also on sister stations owned by Lieberman Broadcasting. The show features a variety of games (including football and name-that-tune), a live band, a little person, and girls dancing in revealing outfits.
