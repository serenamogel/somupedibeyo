Arthur Fry was born on August 19, 1931 in Owatonna, MN and is best known as the co-inventor of the Post-It Note. His invention has become a worldwide phenomenon, with Post-It Notes being used in more than 100 countries. Fry's invention has revolutionized the way people organize their lives and has become an indispensable tool for many.
