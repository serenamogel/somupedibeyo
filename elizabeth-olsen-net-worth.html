Elizabeth Olsen Wiki Biography

Elizabeth Chase Olsen was born on the 16th February 1989, in Sherman Oaks, California USA, however she has English and Norwegian ancestry from her mother`s and father`s sides respectively. She has been an active member of the entertainment industry since 1993.

Elisabeth was born into a family of four siblings, of whom elder twin sisters Mary-Kate and Ashley are also involved in acting. Her net worth increased in 2013, when she appeared in the films “Very Good Girls”, alongside Dakota Fanning in main roles, and in the drama film “Kill Your Darlings”.

Overall, Elizabeth`s career is rather successful, as having started on her own in 2011, she has already appeared in 15 film titles and earned numerous awards, so her career will undoubtedly expand in the coming years, and with these new ventures her net worth will increase.
