Biography

Roshan Pilapitiya was born in Sri Lanka on March 21, 1975. Sri Lankan actor and singer who is known for his recurring roles in several popular series’ including Rajjumala and Dhana Rina. He is also known for his music career and for the popular singles “Dakinna Awe” and “Mage Nisala Lowe”. He starred alongside Saranga Disasekara in the 2018 feature film Nidahase Piya DS. His acting debut came in 1988 when he starred in the teledrama series Golu Hadawatha. He gained further attention in 2002 when he starred in the award-winning film Sooriya Daruwo.
